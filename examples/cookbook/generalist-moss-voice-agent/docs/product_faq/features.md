# Product Features

## Core capabilities
- Real-time semantic search across your documents in under 10 ms.
- Supports text files, markdown, PDFs, and custom embeddings.
- Hybrid search: combine vector similarity (semantic) with keyword (BM25) search.
- Filter results by metadata fields (e.g. category, date, author).

## Integrations
Works out of the box with LangChain, LlamaIndex, LangGraph, CrewAI, and Pydantic AI.
REST and Python/TypeScript SDKs are available.

## Limits (free tier)
- Up to 3 indexes.
- Up to 10,000 documents per index.
- 1,000 queries per day.
Upgrade to a paid plan for higher limits and SLA guarantees.
