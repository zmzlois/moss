# Shipping & Delivery

## Standard shipping
- Domestic: 5–7 business days, free on orders over $50.
- International: 10–14 business days, rates calculated at checkout.

## Express shipping
- Domestic: 1–2 business days, $12.99 flat fee.
- Not available for international orders.

## Tracking
A tracking link is emailed once your order ships. Allow 24 hours after shipment
for the tracking number to become active on the carrier's website.

## Lost or damaged orders
If your package is lost or arrives damaged, contact support within 7 days of
the expected delivery date. We will reship or issue a full refund.
