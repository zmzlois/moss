# Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases.

To request a refund:
1. Contact support within 30 days of your purchase date.
2. Provide your order number and the reason for the refund.
3. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Digital products are eligible for a refund only if they have not been downloaded or accessed.
Subscription plans can be cancelled at any time; you will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
