# Account Help

## Resetting your password
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter your registered email address.
You will receive a reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires after 15 minutes.

## Changing your email address
Log in, go to Account Settings > Profile, and update your email. A verification email
will be sent to the new address. The change takes effect once you click the link.

## Closing your account
To permanently delete your account, go to Account Settings > Danger Zone and select
"Delete account". This action is irreversible and removes all your data within 30 days.
Contact support if you need help retrieving data before deletion.
